List of courses for B.Tech CSE in NIT Andhra Pradesh: 

First Year Courses:
Unless mentioned specifically, the following first year courses belong to semester 1 or 2 for different students.
Differential and Integral Calculus (MA101, 3 credits) - Semester 1
Matrices and Differential Equations (MA151, 3 credits) - Semester 2
ENGLISH FOR TECHNICAL COMMUNICATION (HS101, 3 credits)
Engineering Physics (PH101, 3 credits)
Basic Electronics Engineering (EC101, 2 credits)
ENVIRONMENTAL SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING (CE102, 2 credits)
Introduction to Algorithmic Thinking and Programming (CS101, 3 credits)
Introduction to Algorithmic Thinking and Programming Lab (CS102, 2 credits)
Engineering Physics Lab (PH102, 2 credits)
Physical Education (EA101, 1 credits)
Health Education (EA151, 1 credits)
Engineering Graphics with Computer Aided Drafting (ME102, 2 credits)
Engineering Chemistry (CY101, 3 credits)
Elements of Electrical Engineering (EE101, 2 credits)
BIOLOGY FOR ENGINEERS (BT101, 2 credits)
Basics of Mechanical Engineering (ME101, 2 credits)
Engineering Mechanics (CE101, 2 credits)
Workshop Practice (ME103, 2 credits)
Engineering Chemistry Lab (CY102, 2 credits)

Second Year Courses (Semesters 3 and 4):
Semester 3 (Odd Semester Second Year) courses include -
Data Structures and Algorithms (CS201, 3 credits)
Operating Systems (CS202, 3 credits)
Discrete Mathematics (CS203, 3 credits)
Data Structures and Algorithms Laboratory (CS204, 2 credits)
Operating Systems Lab (CS205, 2 credits)
Probability, Statistics and Stochastic Processes (MA204, 3 credits)
Digital Logic Design (EC237, 4 credits)

Semester 4 (Even Semester Second Year) courses include -
Object Oriented Programming (CS251, 4 credits)
Design and Analysis of Algorithms (CS252, 3 credits)
Computer Organization and Architecture (CS253, 3 credits)
Theory of Computation (CS254, 3 credits)
Database Management Systems (CS255, 3 credits)
Unix Tools and Shell Scripting Lab (CS256, 2 credits)
Database Management Systems Lab (CS257, 2 credits)

Third Year (Semesters 5 and 6):
Semester 5 (Odd Semester Third Year) courses include -
Language Processors (CS301, 3 credits)
Theory and design of Programming Languages (CS302, 4 credits)
Artificial Intelligence (CS303, 3 credits)
Artificial Intelligence lab (CS304, 2 credits)

Semester 6 (Even Semester Third Year) courses include -
Software Engineering (CS351, 3 credits)
Computer Networks (CS352, 3 credits)
Web Application Development (CS353, 3 credits)
Network Programming Lab (CS354, 2 credits)

Fourth Year Semester 7 courses:
Year 4 Semester 1 - Cryptography and Engineering Secure Systems (CS401, 3 credits)
Year 4 Semester 1 - Big Data Engineering (CS402, 4 credits)
Year 4 Semester 1 - Entrepreneurship for Engineers (SM430, 3 credits)
